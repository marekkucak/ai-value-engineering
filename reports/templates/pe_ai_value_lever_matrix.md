## Extensions & Advanced

### For Executives

If you're building a real PE value creation case:

1. **Due Diligence Deep Dive:** Model each top lever with 3-5 year pro forma financials
2. **Risk Factors:** For each lever, identify downside risks (regulatory, execution, market)
3. **Waterfall Model:** Show how levers stack to drive EBITDA bridge (current → exit EBITDA)
4. **Compare to Market:** How does your €X improvement compare to peer companies in same industry?

### For Data Teams

Use Lesson 05 matrix to inform your data strategy:

1. **Data Requirements:** What data do you need to measure each lever? (e.g., for "Faster Sales," you need pipeline velocity data)
2. **Metrics Dashboard:** Build monitoring for top 3 levers (weekly/monthly tracking)
3. **A/B Testing:** Pilot AI on 10% volume; measure impact before full rollout

### For Portfolio Managers (Multiple Companies)

Extend to 5-10 companies simultaneously:

1. **Portfolio Heatmap:** Create matrix of companies × levers to see which levers are universal vs. company-specific
2. **Synergies:** Do insights from Company A apply to Company B? (e.g., if legal AI works in Services firm A, test in Services firm B)
3. **Platform Playbook:** Build reusable AI solutions (e.g., "Support Triage Platform") that applies across 3-5 companies
