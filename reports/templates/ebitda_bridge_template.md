## Instructions

For this exercise, you will build an EBITDA bridge showing how workflow AI optimization translates into company profitability improvement.

**Steps:**
1. Choose or define a company baseline (revenue, EBITDA, margin)
2. Select workflow(s) to optimize (from Lesson 02 or your own data)
3. Stack the bridge: Current EBITDA + Savings − Costs = Pro forma EBITDA
4. Calculate EBITDA delta (€ and %)
5. Connect to enterprise value using a valuation multiple


### Enterprise Value Impact

At typical EBITDA multiples:

| Multiple | Current EV | Pro forma EV | Value Created |
|----------|-----------|-------------|---------------|

| 10x | €15,000,000 | €15,143,900 | €143,900 |

**Conclusion:** At 8x EBITDA multiple (typical for services firms), this AI transformation creates €115k in enterprise value.

## Completed Example 2: Software Company with SaaS Optimization

### Company Profile

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Company Name | CloudSync Analytics |
| Annual Revenue | €25,000,000 |
| Current EBITDA | €5,000,000 |
| EBITDA Margin | 20% |
| Headcount | 180 |

### Workflows Optimized

| Workflow | Baseline Cost | AI Savings | AI Cost | Comment |
|----------|---------------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Customer onboarding | €18,500 | €9,250 | €500 | 50% reduction in manual setup |
| Billing issue resolution | €12,300 | €6,150 | €300 | Fewer escalations |
| **SAAS optimization** | €240,000 | €120,000 | €0 | License consolidation (separate lever) |
| **TOTAL** | **€270,800** | **€135,400** | **€800** | **€134,600 net** |

### EBITDA Bridge Calculation

```
Current EBITDA                          €5,000,000
+ Labor savings (workflows)               €135,400
+ SaaS consolidation savings             €120,000
+ Revenue uplift (from efficiency)             €0
- AI platform cost                         (€800)
- Support/maintenance cost              (€50,000) (more support for SaaS integration)
- Quality risk buffer                   (€20,000)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Pro forma EBITDA                      €5,184,600
═════════════════════════════════════════════════

EBITDA Delta: €184,600
EBITDA Improvement: +3.69%
```

### Notes

This scenario shows:
- **Multiple value levers** (labor savings + SaaS consolidation)
- **Higher support cost** due to SaaS integration complexity
- **Significant EBITDA improvement** (3.69%) justifies implementation investment
- Software companies can realize 2-3x higher % improvement than services due to gross margin leverage
