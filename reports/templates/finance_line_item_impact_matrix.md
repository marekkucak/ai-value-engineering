## Instructions

For each workflow below, identify:
1. **Primary P&L Impact** — Which line item is most affected? (e.g., "Salaries & Benefits," "Software & SaaS," "Revenue")
2. **Secondary Impacts** — Any secondary effects? (e.g., "working capital," "risk reduction," "capacity for growth")
3. **Annual Value** — Quantified impact in €/year
4. **Justification** — Brief explanation of why that line item

---

## Completed Examples (from Lesson 01)

### Example 1: Support Triage Automation
- **Primary Impact:** Salaries & Benefits (OpEx)
- **Secondary Impact:** Capacity for higher-volume handling or revenue growth
- **Annual Value:** €97,192/year (45% reduction in 18,000 annual triage cases @ €55/hr loaded cost)
- **Justification:** Reduces manual triage hours. If headcount is eliminated, direct OpEx reduction. If capacity is redeployed, enables volume growth.

### Example 2: Invoice Approval Acceleration
- **Primary Impact:** Salaries & Benefits (OpEx) + Interest Expense (or Working Capital)
- **Secondary Impact:** Vendor relationships (potential for payment discount negotiation)
- **Annual Value:** €18,313/year (OpEx) + €88,000/year (working capital @ 8% cost of capital for €200M spend)
- **Justification:** 50% faster approvals reduce AP staff time + faster payment frees cash, reducing financing costs.
